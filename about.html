Our Story

Transforming Tourism in Northern Cape

Lelomolarona (Pty) Ltd t/a Lelomolarona Events is a dynamic, community-rooted tourism enterprise based in Mapoteng, Sishen, within the Gamagara Municipality of the Northern Cape. Officially registered in 2022, we commenced operations in January 2023 with a clear and purposeful vision.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to create enriching experiences that not only elevate tourism but also contribute meaningfully to local economic development. We recognized the untapped potential of our region and set out to showcase its hidden gems to the world.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a multifaceted enterprise offering a fusion of tourism and event services, each thoughtfully crafted to provide value, quality, and memorable experiences for our clients while empowering our local community.

Founder & Visionary Leader

Thabang Macdonald Ntshie is the visionary founder and sole shareholder of Lelomolarona (Pty) Ltd. A passionate community developer and events innovator, he brings a wealth of insight, adaptability, and commitment to the tourism industry.

His belief that "our clients' success is our success" drives the business's customer-centric approach. Thabang's multifaceted role encompasses business strategy, client relations, and creative direction.

Hands-On Leadership

Thabang is deeply involved in all aspects of operations, from designing event packages and sourcing local partners to managing bookings, resolving client queries, and ensuring quality standards are met consistently across services.

His leadership has been instrumental in establishing Lelomolarona Events as a trusted service provider despite infrastructural limitations. He leverages limited resources with creativity and resilience, renting temporary accommodation structures and mobilising grassroots marketing channels to maintain a consistent presence in the market.

His willingness to work at every level of the business reflects a strong work ethic and a hands-on entrepreneurial spirit that inspires our entire team.

Community Impact

Empowering Local Communities

At Lelomolarona Events, we believe that tourism should be a force for good. Our community-rooted approach ensures that the benefits of tourism extend beyond our clients to the local economy and residents.

We actively partner with local suppliers, hire from within our community, and develop tourism products that showcase authentic Northern Cape experiences. Our domestic travel packages are specifically designed to highlight hidden gems often overlooked in broader travel circuits.

Through our events and tourism services, we create platforms for local entrepreneurs, artists, and service providers to showcase their talents and grow their businesses.

Our Impact Areas

  • Job creation and skills development
  • Support for local SMMEs and suppliers
  • Promotion of Northern Cape tourism
  • Cultural preservation and celebration
  • Economic empowerment initiatives
  • Sustainable tourism development
