About Us

Transforming Tourism in Northern Cape

Lelomolarona (Pty) Ltd t/a Lelomolarona Events is a dynamic, community-rooted tourism enterprise based in Mapoteng, Sishen, within the Gamagara Municipality of the Northern Cape. Officially registered in 2022, we commenced operations in January 2023 with a clear and purposeful vision.

Our enterprise offers a fusion of enriching experiences that not only elevate tourism but also contribute meaningfully to local economic development. We are committed to inclusive, client-focused solutions tailored to meet the diverse aspirations of our clients.

Our Founder

Thabang Macdonald Ntshie

Thabang Macdonald Ntshie is the visionary founder and sole shareholder (100%) of Lelomolarona (Pty) Ltd. A passionate community developer and events innovator, Mr. Ntshie brings a wealth of insight, adaptability, and commitment to the tourism industry.

His belief that "our clients' success is our success" drives the business's customer-centric approach. Thabang's multifaceted role encompasses business strategy, client relations, and creative direction.

He is deeply involved in all aspects of operations, from designing event packages and sourcing local partners to managing bookings and ensuring quality standards.
