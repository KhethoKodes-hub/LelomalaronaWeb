Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Mapoteng, Sishen, in the Gamagara Municipality of the Northern Cape. While our primary focus is the Northern Cape region, we also serve clients throughout South Africa for our event and tourism services.

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for events during peak seasons. For event advertising and stalls, a minimum of 4-6 weeks' notice is ideal. For accommodation and travel experiences, we suggest booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure availability.

Do you offer customized packages?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating tailored solutions for our clients. Whether you need a custom event package, specific accommodation requirements, or a personalized travel itinerary, we'll work with you to create the perfect experience that meets your needs and budget.

What types of events do you specialize in?

We specialize in event advertising and stall services for corporates, SMMEs, NPOs/NPCs, and business chambers. Our focus is on creating impactful events that promote brand visibility, enhance stakeholder engagement, and drive commercial value through strategic advertising and stall placement.

Can you help with accommodation for large groups?

Yes, we can accommodate groups of various sizes through our temporary housing structures. While we're currently operating temporary facilities, we're planning to transition to NHBRC-standard permanent facilities to better serve larger groups. Please contact us to discuss your specific group accommodation needs.
